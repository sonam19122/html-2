Sonam's Recipe for the best cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instruction

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  3. knead the dough for 10 minutes
  4. Tranfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with the plastiv wrap: leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hr.
  5. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
  6. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
